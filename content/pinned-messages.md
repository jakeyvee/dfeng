## 9. Support & Assistance  (open — msg 35)

```
Welcome to Support & Assistance 🧡

This is the place for anything to do with your car's service, charging, warranty, technical questions, repairs, or general ownership issues. If something's not working the way it should — or you're just not sure who to ask — bring it here.

Posting in this topic helps us route your issue to the right people quickly, instead of it getting lost in general chat. When you can, share a few details (your model and what's happening) so we can help faster.

For everyday chit-chat and model talk, the General Chat and Owners Lounges are the spots — but for anything you need sorted, you're in the right place.
```
