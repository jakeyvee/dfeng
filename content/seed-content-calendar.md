# First-week seed content calendar — Dongfeng Experience Community

**Linear ticket:** VOL-216
**Plan & rationale:** `docs/launch-seeding-plan.md`
**Voice:** `docs/tone-guide.md` — friendly, owner-led, 🧡 accent, never sales-y

A concrete **7-day** plan for the three model lounges + **General Community Chat**
+ **Announcements & Events**. Each fenced block is **ready to paste** by the
assigned person. They're starting points, not a straitjacket — if a real
conversation takes off, follow it instead of forcing the next prompt.

**Ground rules baked into every post:**
- Real person, real detail, open door for others to chime in.
- 🧡 used occasionally as a signature — not every line.
- **Never** ask for a sale, booking, test drive, price, or payment here.
- `$XX` / placeholders mean *the poster fills in their own real number* before
  posting. Don't paste a placeholder.

> **Who posts what** (see plan §2): model-lounge prompts → that lounge's seed
> owner (**TBC**), with Lucia hosting where no owner is named yet and Travis
> picking up service/charging/technical detail. General → **Lucia**.
> Announcements → **Lucia**, ideally L3-signed-off.

## Day 1 — Launch day

### Announcements & Events — *Lucia (L1), L3-signed-off* — Welcome / launch note
```
We're open 🧡

Welcome to the Dongfeng Experience Community — the home for BOX, 007 and VIGO owners (and anyone seriously curious about going electric with us) here in Singapore.

This is an owner-led space. The most useful stuff here won't come from us — it'll come from people who actually drive these cars every day: real charging costs, real road-trip range, the little things you only learn after a few months.

How to find your way around:
```
